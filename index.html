Project Geosim Proposal

About

Title: Geosim

Summary: We hope to create a non-euclidean pathtracer. Our project will create photorealistic renders of geometry in hyperbolic space.

Team Members:

  • Nico A Castaneda (CS 284B)
  • Tianyun Yuan (CS 284B)
  • Jonathan Zakharov (CS184 + CS 284B)

Problem Description

We want to visualize non-euclidean geometry with photorealistic accuracy.

Goals and Deliverables

We intend to deliver a renderer for hyperbolic space (H3).

Baseline Plan:

We will create a renderer capable of visualizing hyperbolic space with global illumination.

Aspirational Plan:

We hope to implement PBR and have stretch goals of implementing more of Thurston's eight geometries and incorporating physics simulation should time permit.
